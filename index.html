Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten is the school year in which kids below the age of 5 begin their journey of their learning and growth.

Elementary School

An elementary school is a school where children are taught for the first six or sometimes eight years of their education.

Middle (10-16) Years

The Middle School comprises Grades 6 through 10 which caters for students between the ages of 11 and 16 years of age.
